\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Holds personal and family health data. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classFamilyHealth}{Family\+Health}} class collects and stores information regarding the user\textquotesingle{}s health and family medical history. It provides methods for gathering input interactively and for retrieving various health metrics.
