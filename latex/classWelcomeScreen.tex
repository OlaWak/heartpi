\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A widget that displays a personalized welcome message and heart health information. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classWelcomeScreen}{Welcome\+Screen}} class displays a welcome message to the user, retrieves and shows heart rate statistics from a CSV file, and embeds a \mbox{\hyperlink{classHeartHealthScreen}{Heart\+Health\+Screen}} to display detailed information and charts.
